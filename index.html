Photography projects by Audrey Bizouerne

Glasgow, April 2020

I’m a photographer and musician, two things I’ve loved from an early age.
Being committed to music and photography inspires me to make connections back and forth between my practices and use insights learned in each to keep learning.
My process is to tell stories without words. From there I then play with rules and logic games to bring out unintuitive elements and challenge my limits.
I like uncalculated smiles and unexpected postures so I work hard on maintaining good relationships with my subjects, starting from a place of comfort so they can forget about the camera and express themselves. That way I can capture the unique and engaging sides of unpredictability.
Being networked among so many fantastic musicians in Glasgow, I chase down every opportunity to work with them and join up my passions.
I moved to Glasgow for music, to take a risk and make a new life playing with committed people. Living in that world gives me the chance to bring out and enhance their personas through photography.
