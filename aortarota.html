TECHNICAL NOTES:


  • Achieving a good reflection in the mirror is not so easy. In the first photo, Raquel wasn't actually looking in the mirror and could neither see me nor herself at all. I'm glad we finally found the right angle by the millimetre giving the illusion that she was looking at herself, and her reflection back at me.
  • It might be a detail but keeping the dress unironed made the texture on the black and white photo interesting and gave depth to the photograph. Just saying for those who hate ironing clothes, there might be a reason not to (am I talking to myself here?).
  • I don't know how many times I used my dark blue Ikea blanket to fake a black background but a lot! DIY again and forever. All these photos were taken in my flat so I could see once again how having to work with limited possibilities created unexpected results. You can even see the edge of the blanket on one photo. I first thought I would cover it but now I quite like it because it looks more authentic.
